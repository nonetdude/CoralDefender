Step 1: Sensing the Water

Using sensors, our model continuously collects essential data such as pH, salinity, temperature, and images to assess water quality and marine ecosystem health in real-time.

Step 2: Data Processing

Collected data is processed through algorithms that detect anomalies, trends, and pollution risks, ensuring users receive accurate and actionable insights into the water conditions.

Step 3: Visualization

All collected and processed data, including images, is presented through intuitive dashboards and user-friendly formats, allowing both experts and the public to easily interpret water health status and take action if needed.

Step 4: Community Awareness

Our model raises community awareness by translating complex data into simple visuals and alerts, inspiring local citizens, schools, and organizations to engage in protecting water bodies and marine life through informed actions.
